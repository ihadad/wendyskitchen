Challah

Ingredients

2¼ tsp. of yeast

4 cups of whole wheat flour

1 tsp. of salt

1 cup of warm water

½ cup of honey or agave nectar

¼ cup of oil

1 egg

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Combine ingredients in a bowl
  3. Let rise
  4. Roll dough into small buns
  5. On a baking tray, form buns into a "pull-apart" loaf
  6. Bake for 25 minutes
