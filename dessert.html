Zucchini Carrot Muffins Carob Chip Cookies Biscotti Rachel's Cookies

Ingredients

2 cups of shredded carrots

2 cups of shredded zucchinis

3 cups of whole wheat flower

1½ cups of brown sugar

1 tsp. of cinnamon

½ tsp. of salt

¾ tsp. of baking soda

1 tsp. of baking powder

1 cup of oil

3 eggs

½ cup of orange juice

½ cup of chocolate chips

1 cup of vanilla protien powder (optional)

12 tsp. of freshly crushed flaxseed

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Combine ingredients in a bowl
  3. Pour mixture into greased muffin tray
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes
  5. Yields 24 muffins (5 gr. of protien per muffin)

Ingredients

1 cup of brown sugar

2 eggs

2 tsp. of vanilla extract

1⅓ cups of margarine

1½ cups of cooked red lentils

3 cups of whole wheat flour

1 tsp. of baking soda

¼ tsp. of salt

1 cup of oatmeal

¼ cup of orange juice

2 cups of carob chips

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Combine brown sugar, eggs, vanilla extract and margarine in a bowl
  3. Add remaining ingredients and mix well
  4. Form cookies and place on an ungreased baking tray
  5. Bake for 12-14 minutes

Ingredients

4 eggs

½ pound of butter

Vanilla or almond extract

2 cups of sugar

6½ cups of flour

2 tsp. of baking powder

1 tsp. of salt

Nuts, chocolate or raisins

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Combine ingredients in a bowl
  3. Form into flattened log on a baking tray
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
  5. After cooling, cut into even slices
  6. Return to oven and bake for another 15 minutes

Recipe by Rachel Hadad

Ingredients

3 eggs

1 cup of brown sugar

¾ cups of oil

⅓ cup of shaved coconut

⅓ cup of sesame seeds

1 tbsp. of water

1 tbsp. of Arak

2 tbsp. of baking powder

Flour

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. Combine ingredients in a bowl, adding flour 1 cup at a time until desired consistency is reached
  3. To shape cookies, roll into thin, finger-length logs
  4. Logs can be joined at the ends for circular cookies, if desired
  5. Place on baking tray and bake for 10-15 minutes
