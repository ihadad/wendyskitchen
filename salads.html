Ingredients

3 or 4 eggplants

¼ cup of oil

3 tbsp. of Vinegar

2 cloves of garlic

¾ tsp. of salt

1 heaping tbsp. of hot paprika

2 tbsp. of fresh chopped parsley

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Slice eggplants ½" thick, leaving skin on
  3. Brush both sides with olive oil and place on baking tray
  4. Bake for 30 minutes on each side
  5. Let cool
  6. To make dressing, crush garlic with oil, vinegar, paprika and salt
  7. Pour over eggplant and garnish with parsley

Ingredients

2 cups of quinoa

3 cups of water

2 green onions, chopped

1 red pepper, chopped

1 stalk of celery, chopped

1 can of chickpeas (optional)

⅔ cup of oil

¼ cup of white wine vinegar

1 mandarin orange

1½ tbsp. of honey

10-12 mint leaves

Salt and pepper

  1. Boil quinoa in water on low heat until all water is absorbed
  2. To make dressing, blend oil, white wine vinegar, mandarin orange, honey, mint, salt and pepper
  3. Toss into quinoa with chopped vegetables

Ingredients

Stuffed green olives

1 onion, chopped finely

Tomato paste

Hot paprika

Chicken stock or soup mix

Tabasco sauce (opional)

Salt and pepper

  1. Soak olives one day ahead
  2. Sauté onion
  3. Add olives and 1 cup of water
  4. Add salt, pepper, 2 tbsp. of hot paprika and 1 tsp. of chicken soup mix
  5. For more heat, add tabasco sauce as desired
  6. Add tomato paste and simmer for 1 hour
  7. As water absorbs, continue adding water 1 cup at a time, up to 3 cups

Ingredients

Carrots

1½ tbsp. of hot paprika

1 heaping tsp. of cumin

1 tsp. of salt

1 heaping tsp. of crushed garlic

2 tbsp. of lemon juice

Olive oil

2 tbsp. of fresh parsley, chopped

  1. Boil carrots until soft
  2. Cool and slice
  3. Combine remaining ingredients in a bowl, using enough oil to create a paste-like consistency
  4. Toss carrots in dressing and add parsley to garnish
  5. Serve at room temperature

Ingredients

2 broccolis

1 cauliflower

¼ cup of mayonnaise

1 tsp. of margarine or butter

½ cup of soy milk or dairy milk

3 eggs

2 tbsp. of flour

2 tbsp. of chicken or vegetable powdered soup mix

Salt and pepper

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Boil broccoli and cauliflower until very soft
  3. Strain out water amd combine with remaining ingredients in a bowl
  4. Mash until puréed
  5. Spray muffin tray with non-stick cooking spray
  6. Fill tray with mixture and back for 45-60 minutes
  7. Yields 18 muffins

Ingredients

5 medium zucchinis, grated

1 large onion, finely chopped

2-3 green onions, finely chopped

1½ cups of flour

⅔ tsp. of baking powder

¼ cup of water

½ cup of oil

3 eggs

2 tsp. of chicken or vegetable powdered soup mix

Salt and pepper

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix all ingredients together
  3. Grease baking dish and fill with mixture
  4. Bake for 1 hour or until golden-brown
