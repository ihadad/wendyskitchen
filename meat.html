Chickpea Chicken Zev's Chicken Spicy Tomato Chicken & Rice Tunisian Kfita Brisket Baked Schnitzel

Ingredients

16 pieces of boneless skinless chicken thighs

¼ to ½ cup of flour

⅛ cup of oil

1 cup of chicken stock

1 can of chickpeas, rinsed and drained

2 tsp. of hot paprika

2 tsp. of cumin

½ tsp. of turmeric

¾ tsp. of black pepper

  1. Heat oil in a large pan
  2. Coat each thigh in flour on both sides
  3. On medium heat, brown half of the chicken on each side for 3 minutes
  4. While browning, sprinkle both sides of chicken with spice mixture
  5. Repeat with second batch of chicken
  6. Combine all the browned chicken with chickpeas in a large pan
  7. Turn heat to low and add chicken stock
  8. Cover and simmer for 25-30 minutes until cooked through

Ingredients

2 chickens, cut with bone in

3 cloves of garlic, crushed

1 cup of oil

1 tsp. of salt

½ tsp. of pepper

1 tsp. of oregano

1 heaping tbsp. of hot paprika

1 cup of flour

  1. Combine garlic, oil, salt, pepper, oregano and paprika in a bowl
  2. Pour half of the marinade over chicken and refrigerate for a few hours or overnight. Save the other half for later
  3. Lightly dip both sides of the chicken in flour and place on an ungreased baking tray
  4. Drizzle the remaining marinade on top of the chicken
  5. Bake at 350°F for 1 hour

Ingredients

16 pieces of boneless skinless chicken thighs

½ cup of flour

¼ cup of oil

2 tsp. of hot paprika

2 tsp. of sweet paprika

2 tsp. of garlic powder

2 tsp. of onion powder

1 tsp. of salt

1 tsp. of pepper

1 large can of diced tomatoes

2 cups of rice

1 tbsp. of powdered chicken soup mix

4 cups of water

  1. Place flour in a freezer bag or bowl
  2. Dip each piece of chicken in flour
  3. Heat oil in a large pan on medium heat
  4. Add chicken
  5. Sprinkle half of the how and sweet paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper on the chicken
  6. Brown for 2-3 minues and turn over
  7. Sprinkle the remaining spices on this side of the chicken
  8. After 2 minutes, pour the can of diced tomatoes over the chicken
  9. Add the rice and water
  10. Add the chicken soup mix and stir
  11. Cook on low for 40 minutes

Ingredients

1 large bunch of parsley, finely chopped

1 large bunch of coriander, finely chopped

1 large bunch of dill, finely chopped

3 onions, finely chopped

3-4 medium-sized potatoes

1 egg

1 heaping tbsp. of salt

1 tbsp. of pepper

1 heaping tbsp. of turmeric

3 heaping tbsp. of hot paprika

½ kilogram of semolina

3 boneless chicken breasts, cut finely (optional)

  1. Boil and peel potatoes
  2. Combine all ingredients in the order above and mix
  3. Form patties and fry both sides in hot oil until brown

Ingredients

Brisket

Dry mustard

½ cup of red wine

½ cup of orange juice

2 tbsp. of ketchup

Pepper

Garlic powder

Sweet paprika

Powdered onion soup mix

Potatoes

  1. Brown brisket in oven at 400°F
  2. Sprinkle with dry mustard, pepper, garlic powder and paprika
  3. Combine wine, orange juice and ketchup and pour the mixture over the brisket
  4. Sprinkle with 1½ packages of onion soup mix
  5. Cover and cook at 350°F for 3 hours
  6. Baste once
  7. Add potatoes later
