Bean & Barley Soup Curry Coconut Soup Pea Soup Lentil Soup Matzoh Ball Soup

Ingredients

Onions

Beans

Barley

Carrots

Celery

Potatoes

Zucchini

Dill

Chicken stock or soup mix

Turmeric

Salt and pepper

  1. Sauté onions
  2. Once browned, add water
  3. Add beans and barley
  4. Add chopped carrots, celery, potatoes, zucchini and dill
  5. Bring to boil and add chicken soup mix
  6. Add salt, pepper and turmeric
  7. Cook on low for at least 1½ hours

Ingredients

Red lentils

Onions

Cauliflower (optional)

Carrots (optional)

Squash (optional)

Sweet potatoes (optional)

Curry

Turmeric

Chicken stock or soup mix

Salt and pepper

Coconut milk

  1. Sauté onions
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Add boiled water
  4. Add red lentils, chopped vegetables (if any), curry, turmeric and chicken soup mix
  5. Boil on low for 30-45 minutes
  6. Lower heat and add 1 can of coconut milk
  7. Stir or purée, if desired
